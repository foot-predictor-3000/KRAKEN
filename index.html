Kraken Archetype Guide

Simple Styles

The Specialist
Relies heavily (>65%) on one specific model (Pattern Scout for NN, Linear Thinker for LR, or Goal Scorer for Poisson).
The Historian
Uses the maximum data range (6 seasons), believing long-term history is the best predictor.
The Modernist
Uses a short data range (2-3 seasons), believing only recent seasons are relevant.
The Trend Chaser
Uses a high recency weighting, putting extreme importance on the last few matches to follow "hot streaks."

Combination Styles

The "Here and Now" Tactician
Combines the Modernist and Trend Chaser styles, focusing only on the most immediate team trends.
The "Long View" Historian
Combines the Historian style with a low recency weighting, trusting long-term, stable patterns over fleeting form.
